Zone Tips

Hover over a red are to see tips for a specific zone (there's a whole 2 zones currently!!!!)

You can also optionally use your eyes and brain to read the text that appears!!!
These tips let you know restrictions in an area to help protect it.

Change Background Color

1. Go to Options tab, and look at the only option there (probably should have called it "Option" instead.)
2. Change color, or reset to default color. It saves change to a cookie that expires in 30 days.
3. Go back to 'Map' tab, and see your astonishing new color (my personal favorite is yellow).
